## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you serve couples outside of Maryland?**
A: Yes. Although based in Bowie, MD, Monica Browne Weddings serves all couples throughout the DMV area — Washington DC, Maryland, and Northern Virginia. Book a call to get started.

**Q: How far in advance should I book?**
A: For full-service planning, 12–18 months before your wedding date is recommended. Shorter timelines can be accommodated.

**Q: How much does full-service wedding planning cost in DC/Maryland?**
A: Full-service planning in the DMV ranges from $7,000 to $25,000+. Partial planning ranges from $3,000 to $7,000.

**Q: How much does a 100-person wedding cost in DC?**
A: A 100-person wedding in Washington, DC costs between $32,000 and $70,000 in 2026, depending on venue, catering style, photography, florals, and entertainment.

**Q: Is it cheaper to get married in Maryland or Virginia vs DC?**
A: Yes. Maryland and Virginia venues typically cost 20–40% less than comparable DC venues.

**Q: What is the cheapest month to get married in DC?**
A: January and February offer the biggest savings — typically 25–35% off peak pricing.

**Q: Is full-service planning only for large weddings?**
A: No. Full-service planning is equally valuable for intimate and micro weddings. The benefit is the level of support and peace of mind, regardless of guest count.

**Q: Can you work with our own vendors?**
A: Yes. While Monica recommends her vetted vendor network, she is open to working with vendors the couple already has in mind, provided they meet her standards.

**Q: Do I need wedding insurance for a DC wedding?**
A: For any wedding over $30,000, yes. DC wedding insurance typically costs $200–$600 and protects against cancellations and vendor issues.

**Q: What is the difference between a wedding planner and a day-of coordinator?**
A: A full-service wedding planner manages every detail from day one — vendor selection, budget, design, logistics, and execution. A day-of coordinator (or month-of coordinator) steps in near the end to manage timelines and vendors on the actual wedding day.

**Q: How long has Monica Browne been a wedding planner?**
A: Monica Browne has been planning weddings since 2004 — over 20 years. She founded her business originally as Uniquely Designed Affairs before rebranding to Monica Browne Weddings.
